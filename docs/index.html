Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Welcome to TicTrek, a premium Tic-Tac-Toe mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user information in TicTrek.

1. Information We Collect

TicTrek is designed to protect your privacy and minimize data collection. Here is exactly what is stored and used:

  • Game Statistics: We track wins, losses, draws, earned trophies, and gold coins. This data is used solely to record your local achievements, calculate your levels, and update shop unlocking states.
  • Local Customizations: Your selected marker skins, unlocked game boards, sound preferences, and vibration settings are saved.
  • Device Storage: All statistics, coins, and settings are saved directly on your device via local storage (SharedPreferences) and are never sent to external servers.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising

To support development and offer free-to-play features (like earning rewards for watching ads), TicTrek integrates the Google AdMob SDK. AdMob may collect and process device-specific information, such as:

  • The advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android).
  • IP address, device info, and carrier details.
  • Ad interaction data to prevent fraud, track performance, and deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

For more details on how Google uses this data, please read the Google Ads & Privacy Policies.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the locally saved data to:

  • Maintain your progress (coins, custom skins, and records) between app launches.
  • Calculate your Trophy levels based on match wins and draws.
  • Optimize app performance, settings, and general gameplay mechanics.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all of your gameplay records are stored locally on your device's private sandbox, we do not retain or store your data on external databases. If you wish to delete all of your data, you can do so at any time by:

  • Clearing the app storage / cache from your mobile device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app, which automatically wipes all local sandbox data.
  • Tapping "Reset All Stats" inside the app's Settings menu.

5. Children's Privacy

TicTrek is a fun and safe game for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal details or track location data of children. Our third-party ad networks (AdMob) comply with COPPA guidelines to ensure safe, age-appropriate advertising practices.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately once posted to this page.
